Senior UX Prototyping Lead

Company: Sony Interactive Entertainment
Location: Tokyo, Japan

About the Role:
We are looking for a Senior UX Prototyping Lead to join our R&D team working on next-generation interactive experiences. You will bridge design, engineering, and product to bring early-stage concepts to life through functional prototypes.

Responsibilities:
- Lead UX prototyping for next-generation interactive products
- Collaborate cross-functionally with engineering, design, and product teams
- Build and evaluate interactive prototypes using modern tools and game engines
- Conduct user research and usability testing on prototypes
- Translate abstract concepts into concrete, testable specifications
- Present findings and recommendations to executive stakeholders
- Mentor junior designers and prototypers

Must have:
- 7+ years experience in UX design or interaction design
- Proficiency in Figma and prototyping tools
- Strong understanding of interaction design principles
- Experience with user research methodologies
- Demonstrated ability to lead cross-functional projects
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills

Nice to have:
- Experience with Unity or Unreal Engine for prototyping
- Knowledge of spatial UI / XR / AR / VR interfaces
- Japanese language ability (JLPT N2+)
- Experience with AI-assisted design workflows
- Background in creative technology or digital art
- Experience with performance profiling and optimization
